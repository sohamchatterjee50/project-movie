About Us

Durgapurians are very fond of Cinema or movies. It is a very popular medium of entertainment and pastimes in Durgapur. Cinema halls of Durgapur get their viewers from not only Durgapur but also from other regions like Panagarh, Rajbandh, Andal, Raniganj and even Asansol. Durgapur cinema is one of the famous Cinema Hall in Durgapur and now the Multiplex Cinema hall giant 89 Cinema has open its Dreamplex in City centre but now taken over by INOX. INox in Dreamplex Durgapur is now taken over by CARNIVAL CINEMAS and will be opened on 3rd April 2015.
